Chapter 1: Introduction to Cricket

Cricket is a popular sport played with a bat and ball between two teams of eleven players each. The game originated in England and has since gained global popularity.

Chapter 2: Basic Rules of Cricket

The basic objective of cricket is to score more runs than the opposing team. Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between the wickets, or hitting boundaries.

Chapter 3: Types of Cricket Matches

There are various formats of cricket matches including Test cricket, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) matches. Each format has its own rules and duration.

Chapter 4: Famous Cricket Players

Many legendary players have made significant contributions to cricket, such as Sachin Tendulkar, Sir Donald Bradman, and Brian Lara.

Chapter 5: Cricket World Cups

The Cricket World Cup is a prestigious international tournament held every four years. Teams from around the world compete to win the coveted title.
